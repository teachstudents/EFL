Before & After Game

Sequence of Events: What happens first?

Before vs. After

BEFORE (Second Event)

Use Before with the action that happens later.

Before you sleep, brush your teeth.

在...之前：Action 2 comes after. (Sleep is 2nd, Brush is 1st)

AFTER (First Event)

Use After with the action that happens first.

After you eat, wash the dishes.

在...之后：Action 1 comes first. (Eat is 1st, Wash is 2nd)

Comma Rule

Start with Before/After = Use a comma.

Before/After in the middle = NO comma.
