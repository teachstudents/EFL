Compound Sentences

The Pattern

Use a comma before the connecting word.

[Sentence 1] , [word] [Sentence 2] .

规则：在连接词（and, but, so, or）前面加上逗号。

Connecting Words

  • and = addition (和)
  • but = contrast (但是)
  • so = result (所以)
  • or = choice (或者)
