Complex Sentences

At the Beginning

If When or While starts the sentence, use a comma!

When [Action 1] , [Action 2] .

如果 When/While 在开头，中间必须加逗号！

In the Middle

If when or while is in the middle, NO comma.

[Action 1] when [Action 2] .

如果 When/While 在中间，不需要逗号。
