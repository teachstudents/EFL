How to use "Because"

Rule 1: The Middle

Use "because" to connect two ideas. Add a period at the end.

[Result] because [Reason] .

规则 1：放在中间。句尾加句号。

Rule 2: The Beginning

If "Because" starts the sentence, you need a comma!

Because [Reason] , [Result] .

规则 2：放在开头。必须要在中间加逗号，句尾加句号！
