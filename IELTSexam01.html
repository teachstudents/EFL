Part 1: Praise & Friends

Examiner Questions: "Do you often tell your friends when they do something well? How do you feel when you receive a compliment?"
Content: Expressed clear opinion? (e.g., "Yes, because...", "I feel happy/proud", "In my view...", "Personally, I think...", "To be honest...", "I definitely believe...", "For me, it is important...")
Grammar (Habits): Used Present Tense? ("I usually tell them...", "It helps them...", "I often say...", "We always check...", "I tend to...", "It makes them feel...", "I try to encourage...")

Part 2: The Story (Narrative Structure)

Examiner Cue Card: "Describe an experience where you did something well. Say what you did, when it happened, and why you felt you did well."
Grammar (Setting Scene): Used Past Continuous? ("I was studying...", "We were working...", "I was preparing...", "The team was practicing...", "She was helping me...", "We were planning...", "It was raining...")
Grammar (Action): Used Past Simple? ("Then I finished...", "I won the game...", "I managed to...", "We achieved...", "I realized...", "It happened...", "They decided...")
Vocabulary: Described the feeling? (Accomplishment, proud, happy, confidence, satisfied, thrilled, relieved, overjoyed, motivated)

Part 3: Recognition & Teachers

Examiner Questions: "Have you ever had an experience where your teacher thought you did a good job?"
Grammar (Reported Speech): Referenced what was said? ("My teacher said that...", "She told me...", "He mentioned that...", "They remarked...", "She pointed out...", "He commented...", "They noted...")
Reasoning: Explained why praise matters? ("It motivates me", "It boosts confidence", "It validates effort", "It shows support", "It builds trust", "It encourages progress", "It makes them smile")

Target Vocabulary Check (B1 & B2+)

B1 (Foundation): Good job, happy, proud, hard work, well done, nice work, good effort
B2+ (Advanced): Compliment, accomplishment, excel at, proficiency, validation
